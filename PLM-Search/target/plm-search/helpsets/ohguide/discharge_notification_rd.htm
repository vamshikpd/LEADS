PC290 Discharge Notification

The PC290 Discharge Notification link is located just below the Step 1 – Case#/Reason section. The PC290 Discharge Notification function provides quick access to parolees with a PC 290 Discharge status. This function allows the user to search by County or by County and City. The results will be displayed on the lower portion of the page and can be sorted and viewed in the same manner as any other search results.

Click the link and the PC290 Discharge Notification dialog box is displayed with the message shown below, the County and City selection drop-down boxes and ability to view Help documentation specific to this function.

The Help icon is displayed in the top right hand corner of the PC290 Discharge. Notification dialog drop-down box.

Click the help icon so you can view Help documentation related to the PC290 Discharge Notification function or the entire LEADS2.0 application.

You must click to select the parolee’s County of residence listed in the selection drop-down box. If you don’t select a county the following error message displays: Error: “Please select a County”.

The lists in drop-down boxes are dynamically created and are data driven. They change when data is modified, entered or deleted from the database.

The County list can change depending on your previous search criteria results.

Click the Search button or press the <Enter> key to activate your search if you elect not to choose a city. The main Search page will reappear and the total number of matching parolees will display on the Step 3 – Parolee Results action bar. Matching parolees are listed below the action bar.

When your results are displayed, the blue Refinements: action bar changed into the PC290 Discharge Search action bar. The section of the action bar that displays the words PC290 Discharge Search is now orange showing you where you created your search criteria. Your search criteria information continues to be listed in blue.

If you know the city and want it to be part of your search criteria, click the parolee’s City of residence listed in the selection drop-down box.
