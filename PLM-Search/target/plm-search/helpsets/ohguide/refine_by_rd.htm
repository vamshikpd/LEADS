Refine by

The Refine by action bar and section are located on under the Keyword Search function. The Refine by section lets you be very specific about the kind of parolee information you need to search for.

You can search three groups of information located as tabs on the Refine by action bar: Personal Information, Parole Data and Vehicle Information.

Personal Information is your default work environment (orange tab) where you can start refining your search. However, you can click the Parole Data or Vehicle Information tab (will turn orange) to change your work environment and search for different type of parolee information.

Each tab allows you to be very specific with your search criteria by selecting or refining data that best fits your need. You can choose information from a variety of data fields or linked data. You can select your search criteria from 1 data field, all data fields or as many as you need to display your desired results. If select none of the data fields LEADS2 displays default information.

Personal Information – you can choose from the following selection data drop-down boxes or linked options:

•            Birth Year

•            Hair Color

•            Eye Color

•            Res(idence) County

•            Res(idence) City

•            Ethnicity

•            Height

•            Weight

•            SMT (Scars, Marks, Tattoos) Code

•            Parolee Age

•            Gender

•            PC290 Reg(istrant) Notification

•            PC290 Discharge Notification

There are 193 Parole Unit/Sub-Unit Offices in 84 different locations within the 4 California CDCR Regions.

Your search criteria results, by default, are sorted based on relevance ranking.

Selection Functions

Your selections dictate how and what data is displayed.

Your first data selection begins the refinement process and displays data based on your search criteria. If you select data from another (your second choice) selection drop-down box, the search function only searches the data that was found for your first selection. If you select from a third drop-down box, the search function only searches the data that was found for your second selection.

Each selection dictates what will be displayed for your next choice.

Example

 

1.                  Chinese ethnicity is your first choice – it lists 210 parolees

2.                  Parolee age 46(+/-3years) is your second choice – it lists 33 Chinese parolees who are 46 yrs old (+/-3years)

3.                  Green eye color – it lists one Chinese parolee, age 46 with green eyes.

Personal Information

Personal information about parolees includes selection drop-down boxes for:

•            the Birth Date (##)

•            CDC # (California Department of Corrections number) (##)

•            Ethnicity (##)

•            Eye Color (##)

•            Gender (##)

•            Hair Color (##)

•            Height (+/- 2 inches) (##)

•            Parolee Age (+/- 3 yrs) (##)

•            Res(idence) City (##)

•            Res(idence) County (##)

•            SMT (Scars, Marks, and Tattoos) Code (##)

•            Weight (+/- 10 pounds) (##)

The pound sign(s) (#) represent the number of parolees who have information in their records related to the subject matter on the list or your search criteria range

The lists in drop-down boxes are dynamically created and are data driven. They change when data is modified, entered or deleted from the database.

 

Example

If you don’t see the Eye Color violet in the eye color list, none of the parolees entered into the database were identified as having violet eyes.

 

Example

The city list can show Sacramento (##) and Scaramento (##). The second city is misspelled and was entered that way by a user and is part of the list until or if the spelling is corrected.

For ease of finding the information, the data fields described below are listed in alphabetical order.

Birth Year - Click the selection drop-down box to select your search criteria from the following list of year ranges:

•            1900-1920 (##)

•            1921-1940 (##)

•            1941-1969 (##)

•            1961-1980 (##)      

•            1981-2000 (##)

Example

You select 1981-2000 (537) – this tells you that 537 parolees were born in that birth year range.

Click the Birth Year drop-down box again to select the parolee’s specific birth year from the list of birth years. The list relates to the parolees born in your selected birth year range. Each Birth Year lists the total number (##) of parolees born that year.

The ‘Matching Records: #,###’ message is displayed on the Parolee Results action bar and all parolees matching your search criteria are listed below it.

The specific Birth Year list can change depending on your previous search criteria results.

When 1 or more birth year is missing from your birth year list, no parolees were born in the non-listed years.

When only 1 parolee appears during your search refinement(s) all options will disappear from and below the Refine by action bar. However, your search criteria will be displayed in the Refinements: information bar (blue).

 

Example

You select the birth year 1991(1) – this shows you that there is only 1 parolee born in that year. All option fields disappear because you no longer need to refine your data.
