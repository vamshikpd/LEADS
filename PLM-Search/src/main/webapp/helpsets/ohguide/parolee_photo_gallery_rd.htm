Photo Gallery

The Photo Gallery tab is located on the top left of the Parolee Detail page. The Photo Gallery is intended to group and display all the photos associated with a given parolee. To access the Photo Gallery select a parolee from the Parolee Detail page. Click the Photo Galley tab.

The photos are displayed as thunbnails on this page and are grouped into three sections Mug Shots, SMT and Other as shown below.

To view a photo, simply click on any available thumbnail. Once a thumbnail is displayed the full size photo will be shown on the left pain.

 

 

Once a photo is displayed on the left pain, it can be printed by clicking the “Photo” button. The entire photo gallery can be printed by clicking the “Gallery”. A speicila Nist format is available for the primary mug shot.
