Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Version LMS 6.0 UZH

1. General Questions

What is LMS?

LMS means Learning Management System; it is an open-source learning platform developed and maintained by the University of Zurich.

What are the requirements if I want to work with LMS?

LMS is a web application. In order to be able to work with LMS you need an internet connection as well as a modern web browser. LMS is optimized for Mozilla Firefox. A valid LMS user account grants full access to all of LMS’s features. When using LMS as a guest without any registration there is only limited access.

Where do I get answers to my questions regarding LMS?

You will get answers to your questions as follows:

  • The Help feature (LMS main navigation, top right) provides you with the LMS user manual.
  • The Help feature also contains a forum. In the section “Discussion” you can ask other LMS users for help.
  • The context-sensitive Help of LMS (indicated by the question-mark icon) explains features of an active LMS page.
  • Your LMS support team can give you further information.

How do I contact the support team?

Please contact your local System Administrator.
