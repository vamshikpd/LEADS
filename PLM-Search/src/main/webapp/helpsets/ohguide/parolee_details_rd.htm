Parolee Details

The parolee detail page will display all information related to the selected parolee.

To view Parolee detail, from the results page select a parolee by clicking anywhere on the parolee record. To return to the search results page click the “<< Back to Results Page” link in the upper right corner of the page.

 The following action tabs and information are displayed (the active tab is always shown in orange) just below the LEADS 2.0 main action bar (LMS, Search, Help, etc...):

•            The Parolee Information tab 

•            The Photo Gallery tab

•            The Photo Line-up tab

•            The Parolee’s First and Last name

•            CDC#

•           “<< Back to Results Page” link (Click the link to return to your search results)

Parolee Information:  

The parolee information is displayed in sections. These sections are listed below in alphabetical order and described in detail (the order of display may not be the same in the application).

•            Alias Information

•            Commitment Offense Information

•            Job Information

•            Moniker Information

•            Other Special Parole Conditions

•            Parole Status Notification

•            Parolee Addresses

•            Parolee Details

•            Quick Information

•            Registration and Notice Information

•            Scars, Marks, and Tattoo Information

•            Special Parole Conditions

•            Vehicle Information

The Alias Information section includes for each parolee, a maximum of four:

•            Alias first names

•            Alias middle names

•            Alias last names

The Parole Status Notification section will display special messages related to parolee’s status or special conditions. For example a parolee with a suspended status (Parolee’s at Large (PAL) will show a notification similar to this one:

Parolee Status Notification messages will vary depending on the parolee.

The Parolee Details section includes:

•            Agent’s name, phone number and email address

•            Birth Date and State

•            Commitment County

•            CII# (Criminal Information Index #)

•            Drivers License #

•            Eye and Hair Color

•            FBI# (Federal Bureau Investigation #)

•            Height

•            Maximum Discharge Date

•            Parole Date and Unit

•            Photograph of the Parolee

•            Revocation Release Date

•            SSA# (Social Security Administration #

•            Supervision Level

•            USINS# (US Immigration Naturalization Service #)

•            Weight

The Quick Information section data fields include:

•            CDC#

•            Ethnicity

•            Gender

•            Name (last, first, & middle)

•            Parole status

Commitment Offense Information section includes data for:

•            Controlling Offense(s)

•            Offense code(s)

•            Description of Offense(s)

Job Information includes:

•            Date

•            Employed or Unemployed

•            City and State where parolee is or was employed

•            Employer Aware(ness)

Moniker Information such as parolee’s other first names, last names or aliases.

Other Special Parole Conditions are noted in this section if they don’t fit in any of the fields within the parolee’s detailed information fields.

Parolee Address(es) information can show you the history where a parolee might be found or with whom the parolee has or still lives. The data fields display in this section include:

•            City

•            Date

•            Map Reference

•            Person Parolee lives with

•            Phone

•            State

•            Street

•            Zip

Registration & Notice Information includes the:

•            Registration codes marked Y(es) or N(o):

•         HS 11590 Drugs

•         PC290 Sex Off(ender)

•         PC457.1 Arson

•         PC3058.6 Violent Felon

•            Reg(istration) Date for these three registration codes:

•         HS 11590 Drugs

•         PC290 Sex Off(ender)

•            PC457.1 Arson

The Scars, Marks & Tattoos (SMT) Information displays:

•            Code/Location(s)

•            Picture(s)

•            Text describing each SMT

Special Comment and Special Parole Conditions include these three conditions marked Y(es) or N(o):

•            Drug Testing

•            No Alcohol

•            Psychiatric Outpatient Clinic
